Iloilo Travel Guide

Your essential guide before visiting Iloilo.

Travel Tips & Information

How to Get to Iloilo

You can reach Iloilo by air through Iloilo International Airport. Direct flights are available from Manila, Cebu, and other major cities. You may also travel by ferry from nearby provinces.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Iloilo is during the dry season (December to May). January is especially popular because of the Dinagyang Festival.

Getting Around

Transportation options include jeepneys, taxis, tricycles, and ride-hailing apps. Renting a car is recommended for visiting resorts and nearby provinces like Guimaras.

Travel Tips

  • Bring sunscreen and light clothing.
  • Carry small bills for public transport.
  • Respect local customs and traditions.
  • Try local food like Batchoy and Pancit Molo.

Must-Experience Events

Don’t miss the Dinagyang Festival in January — one of the most colorful and energetic festivals in the Philippines.
